Cetacean species

This visualization shows a collection of cetacean species. Each circle represents a cetacean, and its position on the scatterplot reveals its size and estimated population. The area of the circles is proportional to the weight of each cetacean, and their color represents their conservation status, according to the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN).

How to read this visualization

Color: Conservation statuses

Area of the circle: Weight of the cetaceans

Select a group of cetaceans

Data sources

  • Wikipedia

Methodology

The population estimate of some cetaceans was provided as a range. In these cases, the average value is shown on the visualization.
